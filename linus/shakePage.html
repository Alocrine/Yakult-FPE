Chocolate Shake

Chocolate milk is made with milk and chocolate syrup (usually made with cocoa powder, sugar, and a bit of water) and a chocolate milkshake is made with milk, chocolate syrup, and ice cream, so it's much richer, thicker, and frostier.

Vanilla Shake

Vanilla Milkshake, world's favorite milkshake, is a smooth and creamy concoction of vanilla ice cream, milk, vanilla extract topped up with whipped cream and chocolate vermicelli for irresistible looks.
