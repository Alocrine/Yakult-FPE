Our Legacy

Purely made from Amadeo soil and raised by Filipino farmers since 1964.
KaffeKo's goal is to promote Filipino coffee or "kape" to local communities.
During 1983, the tea and shake was added to the menu and has been enjoyed by many.

Coffee

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the Coffea genus.

  • Cappuccino
  • Affogato
  • Macchiato

Cappuccino

An espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Austria with later development taking place in Italy, and is prepared with steamed milk foam (microfoam).

Affogato

An Italian coffee-based dessert. It usually takes the form of a scoop of plain milk-flavored (fior di latte) or vanilla gelato or ice cream topped or "drowned" with a shot of hot espresso.

Macchiato

An espresso coffee drink with a small amount of milk, usually foamed. In Italian, macchiato means "stained" or "spotted" so the literal translation of caffè macchiato is "stained” or “marked coffee.”

Tea

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured or fresh leaves of Camellia sinensis, an evergreen shrub native to China as well as other East and Southeast Asian countries.

  • Matcha
  • Earl Grey Tea
  • Mint Tea

Matcha

Matcha is a type of green tea made by taking young tea leaves and grinding them into a bright green powder. Finely ground powder of processed green tea leaves.

Earl Grey Tea

A tea blend which has been flavoured with oil of bergamot. The rind's fragrant oil is added to black tea to give Earl Grey its unique taste.

Mint Tea

Mint tea is a traditional tea that’s celebrated in many cultures, including Morocco. Mint tea is a herbal tea made by infusing mint leaves in hot water.

Shake

A milkshake (sometimes simply called a shake) is a sweet drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, fruit syrup, or whole fruit into a thick, sweet, cold mixture.

  • Mango Shake
  • Blueberry Shake
  • Coffee Milkshake

Mango Shake

A cool and tempting fruit drink prepared by simply blending ripe mango pieces, milk and sugar.

Blueberry Shake

Prepared with frozen blueberries, vanilla ice cream and almond milk tastes heavenly sweet.

Coffee Milkshake

Brewed coffee mixed with cold milk and vanilla ice creams makes an amazing concoction.
